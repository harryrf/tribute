Dimebag Darrell

One of heavy metal's all time greatest guitarists.

Dimebag Darrell playing live with Pantera.

A timeline of Dimebag Darrell's life:

  • 1966 - Born in Annis, Texas.
  • 1981 - Formed Pantera with his older brother Vinnie Paul.
  • 1990 - Saw successful commercial release of debut album Cowboys from Hell.
  • 1992 - Released second album Vulgar Display of Power.
  • 1994 - Released third album Far Beyond Driven.
  • 1996 - Released fourth album The Great Southern Trendkill
  • 2000 - Released final studio album Reinventing the Steel
  • 2003 - Along with brother Vinnie, parted ways with Phil and Rex of Pantera.
  • 2003 - Formed Damageplan with brother Vinnie.
  • 2004 - Damageplan realeases debut album New Found Power
  • 2004 - Darrell is shot dead by a crazed fan while performing live with Damage
  • 1981 - 2004 - Various side projects
